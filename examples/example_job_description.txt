Senior Backend Engineer
Company: Meridian Cloud Technologies
Location: Remote (US)

ABOUT THE ROLE
We are looking for a Senior Backend Engineer to join our platform team and
help scale our core API services. You will design and build distributed
systems that process millions of requests per day, mentor junior engineers,
and collaborate closely with product and infrastructure teams.

RESPONSIBILITIES
- Design, build, and maintain scalable RESTful and gRPC APIs in Python
- Own the reliability and performance of core backend services
- Architect data pipelines for high-throughput event processing
- Write and review code with a strong emphasis on testing and observability
- Mentor junior and mid-level engineers on system design and best practices
- Collaborate with DevOps on CI/CD, containerization, and cloud deployments
- Participate in on-call rotation and incident response

REQUIREMENTS
- 5+ years of professional software engineering experience
- Strong proficiency in Python and at least one modern web framework
  (FastAPI, Django, or Flask)
- Deep experience with PostgreSQL or another relational database
- Hands-on experience with Docker and Kubernetes in production
- Experience with AWS (or another major cloud provider)
- Solid understanding of REST API design and microservices architecture
- Experience with CI/CD pipelines and automated testing (pytest)
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related field, or equivalent experience

PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS
- Experience with event-driven architectures (Kafka, SQS, or similar)
- Familiarity with Terraform or other infrastructure-as-code tools
- Experience with GraphQL
- Prior experience mentoring engineers or leading small technical initiatives
- Contributions to open source projects

WHAT WE OFFER
Competitive salary, equity, remote-first culture, and a strong engineering
mentorship program.
